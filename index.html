About Me (2001)

Hi, I'm Yann Periney, a Game Design student. I've played video games since childhood and always loved them because they would make me discover new and incredible worlds. Over time, I realized I wanted to make games myself and create those incredible worlds. A few years ago, I started learning about Game Design, and it just clicked for me, that this was how I was going to do it. Now, I'm excited to discover and learn more, so I can turn my ideas into playable worlds for people to discover and enjoy!

Welcom to my portfolio, here is a selection of games I did will in school.We had one or two week per project, from a simple game design document to a quick prototype of our ideas. (Document and game are in French)
